Our Solution

Turning textile waste into life-saving infrastructure

RAHA transforms discarded textiles into sterilized, reusable emergency fabric systems. These standardized sheets replace single-use medical textiles with adaptable, multi-functional material that can become slings, masks, wound covers, and thermal wraps on demand.

Why Fabric Sheets?

Instead of disposable bandages, RAHA introduces modular fabric units that can be folded into multiple emergency functions. This reduces dependency on supply chains while improving resilience during crises.
