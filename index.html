Turning textile waste into life-saving emergency preparedness solutions for families, schools, and communities.

The Problem

Textile Waste

UAE generates massive textile waste every year ending up in landfills.

Emergency Gaps

Families lack affordable and accessible emergency preparedness tools.

Our Solution

RAHA transforms discarded textile waste into sterilized, reusable emergency materials such as slings, cooling wraps, masks, and wound covers.
